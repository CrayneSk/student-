ℹ️ About StudentTools Hub

🌍 Built for Students Worldwide

StudentTools Hub is a free, lightweight platform offering essential academic tools for students of all ages. No sign-up, no tracking, no clutter — just fast, useful tools that work offline.

Our Tools: Timetable Generator • GPA Calculator • Assignment Planner • Pomodoro Timer • Notes Organizer

Privacy First: All your data (notes, assignments, timetables) is stored locally on your device using browser storage. Nothing is ever uploaded to a server.

Always Free: Core tools are free forever. Premium features (cloud sync, AI assistant) are optional upgrades — coming soon!
